fxrecord
========

:program:`fxrecord` is a tool for capturing and analyzing recordings of Firefox for
desktop startup. It consists of two parts: :program:`fxrecorder`, which records the
video output of the reference hardware and does analsysis, and :program:`fxrunner`,
which instruments Firefox on the refrence hardware.
